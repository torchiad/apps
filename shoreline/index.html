How we pick your beaches

Every beach gets a match score. Here's exactly what goes in — and what's real vs estimated.

📍 Distance Real

Straight-line distance from your start point to each beach, from real coordinates. Your "drive time" slider widens or narrows how far counts as a good match. Drive minutes are estimated (distance ÷ ~72 km/h), not live routing.

🌊 Water quality Sample now · Real after pipeline

Official bathing-water ratings (Excellent → Poor) from the Environment Agency (England) and Natural Resources Wales. Shown on every beach via the badge and the depth of blue in the picture. Currently typical sample bands; running fetch_data.py pulls the live classifications.

🏖 Quiet vs lively Estimated

There's no public dataset counting people on a beach, so we estimate it from how busy the nearest counted road is (DfT traffic data) and how remote the beach is. Your "vibe" slider matches beaches to how quiet or lively you want.

🦖 Fossils Real (known sites)

Flagged from documented fossil coastlines — the Jurassic Coast (Charmouth, Lyme Regis), Dunraven Bay, the Yorkshire coast and Folkestone. Always search loose on the beach, never dig the cliffs.

🔍 Metal detecting Guidance only

Based on foreshore ownership: ~55% of UK foreshore is Crown Estate (detecting generally allowed between the tide lines), but National Trust beaches ban it and private land needs permission. This is a steer, not legal clearance — always confirm locally before you switch on.
